About Me

Built on Grit,
Driven by Purpose.

I'm Wendell Butler — a business owner, real estate investor, entrepreneur, proud US Veteran, private lender, podcast host, and founder of Hammerhead Capital Inc.

Through my newsletters and podcasts, I aim to deliver expert market analysis, no-nonsense advice, and no-filter investment guidance to real estate professionals and investors out there. I also pull back the curtain on what it actually takes to build businesses: the real grind, the real mistakes, and the real wins. No guru highlights, no polished highlight reels. Just the truth behind building something that lasts.

I'm committed to creating real value in projects and investments, helping investors and partners turn opportunities into high-return deals. By also helping real estate investors secure fast, reliable funding to close deals and scale their portfolios.
