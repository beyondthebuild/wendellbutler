The Man Behind the Work

Built Different.
Built With Purpose.

Wendell Butler is a Charlotte-based real estate investor, entrepreneur, and proud US Army Veteran who has spent over years building businesses that generate real returns and real impact. His path didn't start in a boardroom — it started in the barracks and on the clock, where discipline forged in uniform and the grind of a W2 paycheck taught him the true cost of trading time for money. From military to employee to entrepreneur, Wendell's journey is rooted in sacrifice, mission-first thinking, and the relentless pursuit of something greater than a steady wage.

As founder and president of Hammerhead Capital Inc., Wendell has led the acquisition, renovation, and disposition of 100+ residential and multifamily units across the Carolinas and Northeast (Massachusetts, Connecticut, and Rhode Island) — deploying and originating over $30M in real estate capital. Through Flip Fuel Lending, he provides fast private funding to investors who need to close deals without the red tape. As co-owner of Tide & Timber Ventures, he's now expanding into mixed-use and commercial development across high-growth markets.

"I don't just want to build wealth — I want to build something that means something. Deals that matter. Businesses that last. Communities that thrive."

Beyond the deals, Wendell is the founder of The Hive Group: CLT — a curated entrepreneur community in Charlotte built for people who show up and do the real work. He also hosts Beyond the Build Pod, a no-filter podcast delivering raw, real lessons from the front lines of business and real estate for investors and builders across the country.

The Journey

Wendell's Story

Every milestone was earned — no shortcuts, no overnight wins. Just consistent work and a refusal to quit.

2013

Attended University of Rhode Island

Attended URI studying to become a Physician Assistant — a path I was serious about. Also worked as a Certified Nurse Assistant for some time to start building my PA school application. Got accepted to PA school, but ultimately decided to take a break and explore what else was out there before committing. That break changed everything.

2013

Enlisted in the Rhode Island Army National Guard

Joined as an Infantryman, later advancing to Health Services Officer commissioning through the ROTC program — leading HR and operations for a 200+ soldier medical detachment. Built the discipline, resilience, and leadership that would define everything that followed.

2018

Launched Destined LLC

I started dabbling in entrepreneurship while bartending at the time. Started with just $120 out of pocket. Scaled to $40K+ gross revenue in year one through dropshipping, branding, and self-taught digital marketing — growing to 40K+ social media followers organically.

2020

Joined ADP as Associate District Manager

Won Rookie of the Month twice and led the team in SIMPLE IRA sales for four consecutive months.

2020

Entered the Mortgage Industry

Joined Envoy Mortgage and contributed to $115M+ in originated residential loans across NC, MA, CT, and RI over 3+ years.

2021

Founded Hammerhead Capital

Built a real estate investment holding company around buying, rehabbing, renting, lending, and multifamily stabilization projects — the flagship vehicle for long-term wealth building.

2023

Founded Diabadass

Launched a Type 1 Diabetic and Veteran-owned brand and community turning chronic illness into a superpower.

2023

Loan Origination Manager at Center Street Lending

Moved into hard money and private lending, deepening expertise in fix & flip, rental, bridge, and construction financing.

2025

Co-Founded Tide & Timber Ventures

A residential fix & flip company in the Charlotte metro — leading acquisitions, renovations, and sales completing 15+ flips and new builds in the first year.

2025

Launched Flip Fuel Lending LLC

Providing fast, high-leverage loans to real estate investors for value-add and rental projects.

Present

Continuing to Build

Actively investing, expanding, and building alongside partners at Hammerhead Capital and beyond, with a constant focus on forward momentum and purpose.

What Drives Wendell

Core Values

These aren't words on a wall. They're the principles Wendell operates by in every deal, every conversation, and every dollar deployed.

Capital Discipline

Every move is grounded in structure, underwriting, and honest risk assessment. Discipline kills the bad deals, and clarity closes the right ones.

Transparency & Humor

Honest enough to say it. Grounded enough to laugh about it. No filters, no performance. Just clear truth shared as it is, even when it's uncomfortable.

Community & Collaboration

The best results come from the right people and partnerships that trust each other, move fast together, and compound effort through shared momentum.

Systems Over Hype

Success comes from repeatable systems, not one-off wins or luck. What scales is structure, not noise.

Long Horizon Only

Don't optimize for quick wins. Slower decisions, harder conversations, and a willingness to leave short-term money on the table in exchange for something that actually lasts.
