Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you visit wendellbutler.com (the "Site"), how it is used, and your rights in relation to it.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact form submissions — your name, email address, and message when you fill out the contact form.
  • Newsletter sign-ups — your name and email address when you subscribe via Beehiiv.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used to:

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Cookies

This Site may use cookies or similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience and analyze Site traffic. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features of the Site may not function as intended.

4. Third-Party Services

This Site uses third-party services including:

  • Beehiiv — newsletter subscription management.
  • Calendly — scheduling calls.
  • Cloudinary — media hosting.
  • GitHub Pages — Site hosting.

Each of these services has its own privacy policy and data practices, which we encourage you to review.

5. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and newsletter subscriber information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To make such a request, please contact us at wendell@hhcapitalrealty.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the Site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, reach us at wendell@hhcapitalrealty.com.
